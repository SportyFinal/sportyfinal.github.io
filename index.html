About the Sporty Final

Sporty Final is a powerful application that combines the functionality of an activity tracker, a training planner and a social network for sports achievements. Our app offers a wide range of features:

  • Activity and distance tracking
  • Planning workouts and setting goals
  • Social features for sharing achievements and motivation
  • Personalized recommendations and statistics
